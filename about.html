About me

Aspiring Front-End Developer

I'm an Australian women who made the decision to move to London, UK in 2019 to work and travel.

After obtaining a Bachelors Degree in Science (Biology major), I started to work at a Pathology Laboratory running blood tests as a Biomedical Scientist. After working there for 3 years, I decided to go back to study to obtain my Masters in Biomedical Science (Pathology major). I continued to work as a Biomedical Scientist after my move to London, and have now worked in that field for 7 years. Although I've enjoyed my time working in this field, at the end of 2021, I felt like I needed a change. I found a course online called SheCodes, which teaches women to learn to code. I started the course in March 2022, and have thoroughly enjoyed learning something new, and looking forward to starting a new career as a Front-End Developer.
